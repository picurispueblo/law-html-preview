Pueblo of Picuris Tribal Law and Order Code

12.04.030 Quality Assurance; Testing Requirements.

(a) All dried usable cannabis and all concentrated cannabis derived products produced, sold, or distributed by a licensed producer shall be sampled for testing purposes prior to its distribution to patients. Each batch of dried usable cannabis or cannabis concentrate shall be sampled and tested for;

(1) Microbiological contaminants;

(2) Mycotoxin;

(3) Solvent residue;

(4) Heavy metals; and

(5) Potency.

(b) The following testing procedures shall be followed;

(1) Sampling and segregation of no less than three grams from every batch of harvested dried, useable cannabis, and no less than one gram from every batch of concentrated cannabis- derived product;

(2) Documentation of the sampling and testing of all dried cannabis and cannabis-derived product; and

(3) Remediation, if appropriate, and retesting in accordance with this section.

(c) All medical cannabis cultivated must be tested for patient safety and quality assurance. A cultivation facility may not treat or otherwise adulterate cannabis with any chemical or other compound whatsoever to alter its color, appearance, weight, or smell.
