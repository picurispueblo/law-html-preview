Pueblo of Picuris Tribal Law and Order Code

4.03.070 Payment of Fines and Other Monies.

(a) Fines shall be paid by money order or certified check only, or with the consent of the Court, may be worked off by performing approved labor for the Pueblo of Picuris, at a rate of ten dollars ($10.00) per hour, or as otherwise established by the Tribal Council.

(b) The Court may, upon request of a defendant or upon its own motion, allow that any fine or other required payments be paid in installments and on conditions tailored to the means of the defendant.

(c) The imposition of incarceration should not be imposed as an automatic alternative to payment of a fine or other money, but rather the Court should, upon default to pay a fine or other money as required, examine the reason for the default and may, if justice requires, impose an additional sentence of incarceration of no more than one day of incarceration for each one- hundred dollars ($100.00) of the required money left unpaid.

(d) The methods available for collecting a civil judgment shall be available to collect any unpaid money upon order of the Court following a failure to make any required payment and ascertainment of a reason thereof.

(e) When justice requires, the Court may revoke or remit a fine or any unpaid portion thereof or any other monies required to be paid, or may modify the terms and conditions of payment.
