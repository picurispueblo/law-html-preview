Pueblo of Picuris Tribal Law and Order Code

12.02.050 Registry Identification Card Issuance; Administrative Order.

(a) The Pueblo of Picuris shall establish and maintain a Medical Cannabis Program for the issuance of Registry Identification Cards to qualified persons who meet the requirements.

(b) The Program Manager, or a designee, shall issue a Registry Identification Card to a person who is a resident of the Pueblo of Picuris, a resident of any Pueblo or Tribe in New Mexico, or a resident of the State of New Mexico, or a person with a verified Nonresident Medical Cannabis Card who submits an application on a form prescribed by the Program Manager accompanied by all documents required by this title.

(c) The Registry Identification Card is an Administrative Order of the Medical Cannabis Program and shall be upheld by the Tribal Court and acknowledged and enforced as such.

(d) Upon receipt of the Application for a Medical Cannabis Registry Identification Card, the Program Manager shall:

(1) Record on the Application the date it is received;

(2) Retain the original Application and make two copies for the file;

(3) Review the contents of the Application and verify information accordingly;

(4) Identify any incomplete or missing items in the Application and notify the applicant of said review and decision;

(5) Recommend the approval or denial of the Application; and

(6) Issue or deny the Registry Identification Card, with written notice provided.
