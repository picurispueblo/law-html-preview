Pueblo of Picuris Tribal Law and Order Code

11.01.290 Special Provisions for Tribal Departments and Agencies:

In any case in which the Pueblo of Picuris Tribal Council or any Tribal agency or department is alleged to have violated the terms and conditions of a solid waste permit, or to have conducted activities without a permit, the Pueblo of Picuris Environment Department shall bring the matter to the attention of the Governor of the Pueblo of Picuris who shall consider taking action to ensure compliance with this Ordinance.
