Pueblo of Picuris Tribal Law and Order Code

1.06.030 Trial Juries

(a) The Court Clerk shall subpoena not less than fourteen (14) persons from the list of eligible jurors to appear and be available to serve as jurors whenever a jury trial is scheduled in a civil or criminal matter.

(b) The selection from the list of eligible jurors shall be by lot or some other means of random, impartial selection.

(c) Selection of jurors to hear the case shall be accomplished as provided in the Rules of Civil and Criminal Procedure found in Title 2 and Title 3 in this Tribal Code.
