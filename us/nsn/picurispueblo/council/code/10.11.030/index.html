10.11.030 Permanency Planning Hearing; Disposition.

Within twelve (12) months of the original placement and every three (3) months thereafter, the Court shall hold a permanency planning hearing in accordance with the Children's Code to determine the future status of the minor. Recommendations made as a result of the administrative review shall be presented to the Court at this hearing and the Court shall adopt the recommendations unless good cause to the contrary is shown. The permanency planning hearing may be combined with the periodic review. The Court may order, but is not limited to, any of the following dispositions:

(a) Returning the minor to the parent(s);

(b) Ordering that a guardian be appointed and placing the child with the guardian;

(c) Continuing the minor in foster care for a specific period;

(d) Continuing the minor in foster care on a permanent or long term basis; and

(e) Recommending parental rights be terminated and the minor placed for adoption.
