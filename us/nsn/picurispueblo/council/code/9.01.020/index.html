Pueblo of Picuris Tribal Law and Order Code

9.01.020 Definitions.

"All terrain vehicles" means any motorized vehicle designed for, or capable of travel over unimproved terrain;

"Aquatic wildlife" means any species of fish, crustaceans, aquatic insects, or amphibians;

"Bag limit" means the maximum number of game animals which may be lawfully taken by an individual in a given period;

"Big game animals" means all species of deer, elk, bighorn sheep, American pronghorn, bear, and mountain lion;

"Carcass" means the body of, or parts of a dead animal;

"Certificate of registration" means a document granting authority to engage in activities not covered by a license, permit or tag issued under this Code or by proclamation of the Tribal Council;

"Chumming" means the introduction of bait usually consisting of parts of fish into the water to attract fish;

"Closed season" means that period of time during which the taking of wildlife is prohibited; "Code" means the Pueblo of Picuris Game and Fish Code;

"Department" means the Pueblo of Picuris Parks and Wildlife Department;

"Director" means the Director of the Pueblo of Picuris Parks and Wildlife Department;

"Firearm" means pistols, revolvers, rifles, shotguns and any other device from which a projectile is expelled with the use of gunpowder;

"Fishing" means any method of taking fish from the waters. The Pueblo of Picuris may determine the proper method or methods for taking of fish from the waters of the Pueblo, and may regulate the taking of fish from waters within its boundaries no matter what type of method is used;

"Game fish" means all species of trout, perch and pike; "Hunting" means the activity or sport of pursuing game;

"License" means the primary document granting authority to engage in activities covered by this Code;

"Non-Tribal member" means all persons who are not enrolled members of the Pueblo of Picuris;

"Officer" means an officer of the Pueblo of Picuris Parks and Wildlife Department or BIA law enforcement;

"Open season" means that period of time during which the taking of wildlife by legal means is permitted;

"Permit" means a secondary document, requiring a license as a prerequisite to its issuance, which grants authority to engage in certain specified activities under this Code or by proclamation of the Tribal Council;

"Pueblo of Picuris Indian Lands" means any land within the exterior boundaries of the Pueblo of Picuris and any waters within the boundaries shall be considered "Indian Land". The term "Indian Land" includes the Pueblo of Picuris and the exterior boundaries as well as any land purchased by, leased by, or otherwise subject to the control of the Pueblo of Picuris;

"Possession limit" means the maximum number of fish or wildlife which may be possessed by an individual at any one time;

"Proclamation" includes the rules and regulations of the Tribal Council adopted and published under the provisions of this Code;

"Protected species" shall include such species as the Pueblo of Picuris Parks and Wildlife Department shall declare and designate, unless altered by regulation or proclamation;

"Pueblo" means the Pueblo of Picuris;

"Ranger" shall mean an officer of the Pueblo of Picuris Parks and Wildlife Department;

"Recreational Vehicle" means a motor vehicle or trailer equipped with living space and amenities found in a home;

"Sell" means offer or possess for sale, barter, exchange or trade, or the act of selling, bartering, exchanging or trading;

"Small game animals" means all species of rabbits, squirrels, bobcats, coyotes, and foxes;

"Tag" means a card, label or other identification device issued for attachment to the carcass of any wildlife prior to transport;

"Take" means to fish, angle, hunt, pursue, catch, capture, trap, kill or possess any wildlife or fish or any attempt to commit any of these acts;

"Tribal member" or "member of the Tribe" means any enrolled member of the Pueblo of Picuris;

"Tribe" means the Pueblo of Picuris;

"Wildlife" means any form of animal life generally living wild in a state of nature, including all wild mammals, birds, fish, reptiles, and amphibians and their nests, eggs and spawn.
