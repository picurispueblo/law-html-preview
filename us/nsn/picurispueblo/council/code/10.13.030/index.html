Pueblo of Picuris Tribal Law and Order Code

10.13.030 Petition.

A petition for adoption shall be filed with the Court. It shall be verified under oath by the petitioning party and shall contain:

(a) The full name, residence and place of birth, date, and sex of the child, with attached documentary proof of the date and place of birth of the child to be adopted;

(b) Documentary proof of the child's enrollment with the Tribe;

(c) The full name, residence, date and place of birth, occupation of the petitioning party, statement of relationship to child, with attached documentary proof of their marital status, provided that this not be interpreted to prohibit single parent adoptions;

(d) Proof of parental consent to the adoption where the petitioners are relatives of the child either by blood or affinity, except in cases where the natural parents have abandoned the child and cannot be located. In cases where there is documentary proof of a court order terminating the parent-child relationship with respect to each living parent of the child to be adopted;

(e) An agreement by the adopting parent that it is his/her desire that the relationship of parent and child be established between them and the child;

(f) A full description and statement of value of all property owned, possessed or held in trust by and for the child;

(g) A citation to the specific Section, including number of this Code which gives the Court jurisdiction of the proceedings; and

(h) A brief and concise statement of the facts, which may aid the Court in its determination.
