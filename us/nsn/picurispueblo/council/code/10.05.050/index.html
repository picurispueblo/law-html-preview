Pueblo of Picuris Tribal Law and Order Code

10.05.050 Adults.

(a) Parental Responsibility.

(1) Parent as Party: The parent of the child may be made a party to a petition if the child is alleged to be a minor-in-need-of-care or a juvenile offender.

(2) Dispositions: The Court may order the parent to submit to counseling, participate in any probation or other treatment program ordered by the Court and, if the child is committed for institutionalization, to participate in any institutional treatment or counseling program including attendance at the site of the institution.

(3) Fines: The parent is not liable for a fine his/her child may be ordered to pay.

(4) Cost of Support: The Court shall order the parent to pay the reasonable cost or part of the cost of support and treatment of the child if:

(A) The child is adjudicated a minor-in-need-of-care, or a juvenile offender; and

(B) The Court orders this child placed with an agency, institution or an individual other than the parent.

(b) Other Adults: The Court shall join as a party any adult necessary for proper disposition of any case heard pursuant to this Code.

(c) Enforcement: The Court may enforce any orders issued under this section by use of its contempt power.
