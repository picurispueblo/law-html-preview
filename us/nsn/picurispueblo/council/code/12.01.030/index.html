Pueblo of Picuris Tribal Law and Order Code

12.01.030 Authority to Establish Medical Cannabis Program.

(a) This title hereby authorizes the Pueblo of Picuris to establish a Medical Cannabis Program to meet the medical needs of tribal members and qualified patients, and to ensure an adequate supply of medical cannabis for the number of patients in the registry, and to provide the right to use and possess cannabis for medical purposes when recommended by a medical provider who has determined that the person's health would benefit from the use of cannabis in the treatment of a debilitating medical condition.

(b) In order to further the goals and to provide an additional source of governmental revenue, the Tribe has adopted this title, which shall be liberally construed to fulfill the purposes for which it has been adopted.

(c) The Medical Cannabis Program shall have an Executive Committee for administrative decision-making as specified herein, and to promulgate Program Rules as necessary, for adoption by the Tribal Council.

(d) The Medical Cannabis Program shall hire a Program Manager to fulfill the administrative purposes and achieve program implementation who shall report to the Executive Committee for supervision.

(e) Nothing in this title shall be deemed to be in conflict with the Controlled Substances Act, 21 U.S.C. section 801 et seq. or to otherwise permit any activity that is lawfully prohibited under that Act.

(f) Nothing in this title shall be deemed to create a right for individuals to grow and distribute cannabis.

(g) Nothing in this title shall be construed to (1) allow persons to engage in conduct that endangers others or causes a public nuisance; (2) allow the diversion of medical cannabis to anyone other than the registered cardholder; (3) allow any activity relating to the cultivation, distribution, or consumption of cannabis that is otherwise illegal.

(h) The Medical Cannabis Program shall operate to (1) prevent the distribution of cannabis to persons under 21 years of age; (2) prevent the revenue from the sale of cannabis from going where it is legal to other states; (4) prevent cannabis activity that is legal under this title from being used as a cover or pretext for the trafficking of illegal drugs or other illegal activity; (5) prevent violence and the use of firearms in the cultivation and distribution of cannabis; (6) prevent drugged driving and the exacerabation of other adverse public health consequences associated with the use of cannabis; (7) prevent the growing of cannabis on public lands and the attendant public safety and environmental dangers posed by cannabis production on public lands; and (8) prevent the possession and use of cannabis on federal property.
